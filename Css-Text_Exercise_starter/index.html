leafy seadragon

The leafy seadragon (phycoduruseques) is the only member of the genus Phycodurus and is a marine fish in the family Stngnathidae whihc includes seadragons, pipefish, and seahorses.[2]

Description

Much like the seahorse, the leafy seadragon's name is derived from its resemblance to another creature (in this case, the mystical dragon). While not large, they are slightly larger than most seahorses, growing to about 20-24 cm (8-9.5 in). They fed on plankton and small crustaceans.
